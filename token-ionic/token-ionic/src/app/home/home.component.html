The benefits of SETU to the government and its citizens

Whenever a common person needs to retrieve or obtain a document or certificate from any division/department of the government then he or she has to go through a lot of hassles. Waiting in the long queue and dealing with officials are the two prime issues. But, with the inception of SETU, these issues can be removed, as the certificate or document can be accessed easily with a few clicks of a mouse. On the other hand, it will help the government to speed up its work process and bring in more transparency is its system. The process of obtaining any certificate or document through the online system has been kept quite simple, and the aim of the government is to issue the document/certificate within a time span of 15 days.
